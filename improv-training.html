Unscripted Genius: Leading Tech Teams with Wit and Wisdom

A Five Module Program for Engineering Managers

Unlock Your Team's Potential

This unique training program is designed for engineering managers in tech, focusing on improvisational comedy techniques to enhance leadership skills.

What You Will Gain

Participants will gain:

  • Enhanced Communication Skills: Learn to express ideas more clearly and effectively, fostering an environment of open dialogue.
  • Boosted Adaptability: Develop the ability to think on your feet and adapt to changing circumstances with confidence.
  • Fostered Creativity: Unlock creative thinking and innovative problem-solving through dynamic exercises.
  • Learning to Fail and Accept It: Embrace setbacks as opportunities for growth, building resilience.
  • Building a Culture of Positivity: Cultivate a supportive atmosphere that encourages risk-taking and collaboration.

TRAINING OUTLINE

Introduction to Improv in Leadership

  • Overview: Discover how the foundational principles of improv related your day-to-day business reality.
  • What We Do: Engage in introductory improv exercises to break the ice and illustrate the power of spontaneity.
  • Takeaways: Gain an understanding of how improv techniques can be applied to foster a more dynamic and adaptable team enviroment.

Module 1: Communication Mastery through Improv

  • Core Rules for Communication in Improv: Learn the essential principles such as "Yes, And..." to encourage positive and constructive exchanges.
  • Active Listening in Practice: Participate in exercises designed to enhance listening skills, ensuring you're fully engaged with your team.
  • What We Do: Hands-on activities that emphasize the importance of clarity and empathy and acceptance in communication.
  • Takeaways: Improved ability to listen, understand, and respond effectively in any professional situation.

Module 2: The Art of Adaptability

  • Embracing Change: Explore improv exercises that mimic rapidly changing scenarios, teaching you to adapt swiftly and efficiently.
  • Pivoting with Confidence: Learn techniques to pivot ideas and strategies seamlessly in response to new information.
  • What We Do: Scenario-based activities that challenge participants to think and act quickly.
  • Takeaways: A toolkit for navigating uncertainty and turning challenges into opportunities.

Module 3: Unleashing Creativity and Innovation

  • Creative Thinking Exercises: Use improv games to stimulate out-of-the-box thinking and generate innovative ideas.
  • Building an Innovative Mindset: Foster an environment where creativity is encouraged and celebrated.
  • What We Do: Group brainstorming sessions facilitated through improv, promoting creative solutions.
  • Takeaways: Strategies to inspire and maintain a high level of creativity within your team.

Module 4: Building and Sustaining Team Dynamics

  • Trust and Support in Improv: Participate in exercises that build trust and mutual support, key components of effective teams.
  • Collaborative Storytelling: Work together to create stories, highlighting the importance of each member's contribution.
  • What We Do: Activities focused on enhancing cooperation and understanding among team members.
  • Takeaways: Improved team cohesion and the ability to work effectively towards common goals.

Module 5: Embracing Failure as a Stepping Stone

  • The Improv Approach to Failure: Understand how improv views mistakes as opportunities for learning and growth.
  • Reframing Setbacks: Engage in exercises that teach resilience and the positive reframing of failures.
  • What We Do: Scenarios that simulate setbacks and encourage constructive responses.
  • Takeaways: A mindset shift towards viewing challenges as essential steps in the learning and innovation process.

Who Should Attend

Engineering Managers, Tech Leaders, Engineering Directors, Project Managers
